About Club96 Fitness

Our Mission & Philosophy

At Club96 Fitness, our mission is to empower individuals to achieve peak physical and mental well-being through personalized fitness journeys. We believe in a holistic approach, combining effective workouts, expert guidance, and a supportive community to help you transform your life.

We pride ourselves on providing a clean, hygienic, and motivating environment. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art, imported premium equipment, ensuring you have access to the best tools for your workout. Our certified, experienced, and friendly trainers are dedicated to offering personalized coaching, crafting workout plans tailored to your unique goals, whether you're a beginner or an advanced athlete. We also provide comprehensive diet planning support to complement your training.

  • Modern Imported Equipment: Experience world-class fitness with our premium machinery.
  • Expert Certified Trainers: Learn from the best and achieve your goals safely and effectively.
  • Personalized Coaching: Tailored workout and diet plans for every individual.
  • Supportive Community: Train in an encouraging and friendly atmosphere.
